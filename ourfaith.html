Holy Orthodoxy

The Orthodox Church

  As Orthodox Christians we stay true to the traditions of the church fathers, the 7 ecumenical councils, and the Nicean Creed. We venerate, but do not worship, our church fathers and saints with iconography. We believe our Lord and Savior Jesus Christ the Son of God was born of a virgin died on the cross for our sins and was risen in three days. We understand the Church as the bride of Christ and our purpose is to fulfill our God given role withing the church until our Lord returns.
