About Us

Community

Our Church has been and remains an Orthodox Christian and Military community dating back to our origins. As Orthodox Christians we stay true to the traditions of the Nicean creed and venerate our church Fathers and Saints. Our church is open to all who come in good faith and would like to participate in the body of Christ. We frequently hold events, potlucks, and the like and treat each other as family. We encourage all newcomers to come and join us for Divine Liturgy on Sundays at 08:00 AM and try it out for yourselves!
