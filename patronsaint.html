Goerge of Lydda

  Saint George, also George of Lydda, was a soldier of Cappadocian Greek origins, member of the Praetorian Guard for Roman emperor Diocletian, who was sentenced to death for refusing to recant his Christian faith.

"You can kill us but you cannot do us any real harm"
-Saint Justin

  As Soldiers and Christians we understand we have nothing to fear besides the Lord. This fearlessness and defense of our faith is a perfect portrayal of how we should strive for our attitudes to become.
