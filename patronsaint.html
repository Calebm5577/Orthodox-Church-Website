St. George
George of Lydda

Goerge of Lydda

Saint George, also George of Lydda, was a soldier of Cappadocian Greek origins,
member of the Praetorian Guard for Roman emperor Diocletian,
who was sentenced to death for refusing to recant his Christian faith.
He became one of the most venerated saints and megalomartyrs in Christianity,
and he has been especially venerated as a military saint since the Crusades.

"You can kill us but you cannot do us any real harm"
-Saint Justin
